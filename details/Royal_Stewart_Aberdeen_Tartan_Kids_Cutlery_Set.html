Royal Stewart Aberdeen Tartan Kids Cutlery Set

This adorable kids cutlery set is specifically designed for toddlers, combining charm with practicality. Featuring a charming tartan pattern, it adds a touch of style to everyday mealtime. The set includes a fork, spoon, and knife, all made from high-quality, BPA-free stainless steel that is both rust-resistant and long-lasting. Whether your child is learning to feed themselves at home, in school, or on the go, these utensils are built to keep up with their growing independence.

The ergonomic handles are perfectly sized for little hands, making it easier for toddlers to grip and use their utensils comfortably. The rounded edges and short tines are thoughtfully designed for safety, ensuring a worry-free experience for both parents and children. With a compact and durable storage case, this portable set is easy to carry in backpacks or lunch bags, keeping everything clean and organized. Although it's not dishwasher safe, it's simple to maintain and retains its vibrant color and shine for years to come.
